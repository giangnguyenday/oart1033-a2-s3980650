hoa

photoroman/davinciresolve/lightroom/life&death

"Hoa," a short movie, is a mesmerizing compilation of photographs that takes us through the fleeting life of a flower. In just a few seconds, we see the flower bloom and wilt, much like our own journey from birth to death. The movie serves as a beautiful reminder of the beauty and brevity of life that we often take for granted.

The use of three main colors - green for youth, blue for mid-age, and red for death - to depict the flower's state adds a unique touch to the visual storytelling. The visuals are further synchronized with the audio to create a thought-provoking storyline that leaves a lasting impact on the viewer.
