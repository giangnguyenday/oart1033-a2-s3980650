Feesh

icon/illustrator/creature

FEESH is a set of icons that centered Nature Biodiversity, specifically, deep-sea animals.

The deep-sea, also known as the Hadal zone, presents a unique and challenging environment for its inhabitants. The creatures that thrive in this extreme habitat have adapted in dramatic ways, transforming their appearances and expanding their abilities to survive in a world unlike any other. These adaptations make them appear like mutants compared to their counterparts living closer to the water's surface, but they are truly remarkable "diamonds" that have been forged under immense pressure. As George S. Patton Jr once said, "Pressure makes diamonds," and these creatures are the shining embodiment of that sentiment - tough, resilient, and truly special.
