ABOUT

Despite being a newbie in design, I have grown my passion for it through drawings and sketches since I was a toddler. While I may be new to the formal design practice, the creative process is not foreign to me. I have spent countless hours experimenting with different mediums, exploring various artistic styles, and pushing the boundaries of my imagination. This artistic background has shaped my keen eye for detail, aesthetics, and the ability to communicate ideas visually. I am excited to embrace this creative journey, widen my ability, and make my mark in the design industry.
