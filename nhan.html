nhàn

eventwebsite/illustrator/figma/musicfestival

This is a group project in which I participated in the illustration and prototyping part. “Nhan” is a creative fictional event website designed to immerse audiences in the captivating world of Hue royal court music.

Through Hue Royal Concerts performed by animal singers associated with the spiritual life of Vietnamese people, we aim to narrow the generation gap by connecting young people with a rich tapestry about our folk culture. Immerse yourself in an enchanting experience that not only preserves Hue's heritage but also inspires a new appreciation for our cultural heritage.
