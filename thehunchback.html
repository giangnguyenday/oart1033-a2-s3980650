The Hunchback

click to experience

interactivewebpage/illustrator/poem

This project will explore the ‘The Hunchback’ poem by John Peale Bishop as a web page. The poem describes the loneliness and solitude of a hunchback who failed to find love and careness from others; Moreover, it also highlights how harsh and cruel the reality is to the forgotten people.

‘The Hunchback’ will give the audience a pessimistic view and dark deep insights. Not only with visuality but also audio, this project will bring an engaging experience for the audience.
